Core Features

Powerful, configurable options for users who demand precision and reliability.

Silent Aim
Subtle targeting adjustments designed for smooth, unobtrusive aim correction.
Aimbot (Camlock, Mouse Lock)
Camera lock and mouse lock modes with adjustable smoothness and target prioritization.
WalkSpeed
Fine-grained movement speed control for testing and mobility tuning.
ESP (Boxes, Healthbar, Names, Item)
